The Traditional Indian Tea

    Ingredients:

    • 1 tea cup of water
    • 2 tablespoon of tea leaves
    • 1 tablespoons ginger
    • 2 tablespoon of sugar (according to taste)
    • Half tea cup of milk

    Instructions

    • Boil Water: Bring a pot of water to a boil and heat it for at least 2 minutes at high flame.
    • Add Sugar and Tea Leaves: After water is boiled add sugar and tea leaves.
    • Add Ginger: After adding sugar wait for 30 second and add grated ginger.
    • Add Milk:After adding ginger add milk.if the milk will be boiled the tea will be made faster.
    • Boil the tea:After all ingredients are added leave the tea at low flame for 5 minutes.
    • Serve:Remove from heat and serve immediately.
