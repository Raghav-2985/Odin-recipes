The Classical Pasta

  1. Ingredients:
    • 1 (8 ounce) package dry spaghetti
    • 8 tablespoons butter
    • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
    • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
    • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
    • 1 pinch cayenne pepper to taste (optional)
    • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • Cream (optional, to taste)

  2. Instructions
    • Boil Water: Bring a pot of water to a boil and add salt. Cook the spaghetti according to package directions until it is al dente (tender yet firm).
    • Drain Pasta: Once cooked, drain the pasta and set it aside.
    • Saut√© Garlic: In a medium pan over medium heat, melt the butter. Add the minced garlic and stir until it becomes fragrant, which should take about 1 to 2 minutes.
    • Add Broth and Seasoning:Pour in the chicken broth and bring it to a boil. Add salt, black pepper, and cayenne pepper (if using) to taste.
    • Combine Ingredients: Add the cooked spaghetti to the pan with the broth mixture. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the pasta has absorbed some of the liquid and is thoroughly combined with the sauce (about 12 minutes, though timing may vary).
    • Finish the Sauce: Remove from heat. Stir in the Parmesan cheese, parsley, and cream (if using).
    • Serve: Mix well and serve immediately.
